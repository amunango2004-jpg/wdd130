History

White Water Rafting was founded in 2005 with the goal of providing the best white water rafting experiences in the region. Over the years, we have grown to become the most trusted name in white water rafting.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include a variety of rafting experiences for all skill levels, from beginners to experienced adventurers.
